# Qualification Scorecard (Mercury)

Score each prospect 0-10. Prioritize totals >= 7 for wave 1 outreach.

## Criteria
1. **Recent Activity (0-2)**
- 2: active in last 7 days
- 1: active in last 30 days
- 0: stale/no activity

2. **Pain Fit (0-2)**
- 2: explicitly mentions lead quality, conversion, or ops bottlenecks
- 1: adjacent pain
- 0: no visible pain

3. **Budget Signal (0-2)**
- 2: sells products/services and actively monetizing
- 1: unclear monetization
- 0: hobby/non-commercial

4. **Channel Access (0-2)**
- 2: open DMs or direct contact available
- 1: indirect channel only
- 0: no contact path

5. **A2A Fit (0-2)**
- 2: clearly uses agents/automation already
- 1: early experimentation
- 0: no evidence of agent usage

## Decision Rules
- **8-10:** HOT outreach now
- **6-7:** WARM queue after first wave
- **0-5:** defer unless strategic logo
