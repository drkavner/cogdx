# Mercury A2A Offers v1

## 1) /lead_score
**Outcome:** Prioritize leads most likely to convert in the next 7 days.
- Input: lead context, source, intent signals
- Output: score (0-100), rationale, next best action
- Price: $0.20/call (pilot), $0.35/call (standard)

## 2) /close_message_draft
**Outcome:** Generate high-conversion async close messages without calls.
- Input: lead profile, objections, prior messages, offer
- Output: 3 close variants + objection handling
- Price: $0.30/call (pilot), $0.50/call (standard)

## 3) /content_angle_rank
**Outcome:** Rank content hooks by conversion potential.
- Input: niche, offer, audience, recent posts
- Output: ranked angles + CTA recommendation
- Price: $0.25/call (pilot), $0.40/call (standard)

## Pilot Terms (14 days)
- $99 pilot minimum spend
- Usage-based overage
- Success metric agreed before start
- Auto-convert to paid on KPI hit
